Welcome to MediCare Hospital

Mini Hospital Management System is a modern healthcare platform designed to simplify hospital operations, improve patient care, and provide an efficient experience for doctors, patients, and administrators.

About Our System

Our Hospital Management System digitizes hospital workflows, allowing healthcare professionals to manage appointments, prescriptions, patient records, and doctor schedules efficiently through an intuitive interface.

Built using Django, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL, and modern web technologies, the platform ensures security, reliability, and an excellent user experience.
